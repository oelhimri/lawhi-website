How it works

Learning by writing, reimagined

For centuries, students of the Quran learned by writing on a lawh — a wooden tablet. Lawhi turns that tradition into a focused, modern practice.

STEP 01

Start with a blank page

Each page begins empty, with only the verse markers in place. A word tray slides up from the bottom, holding every word on the page in alphabetical order.

١
STEP 02

Rebuild it word by word

Choose the right words in the right order to reconstruct the page from memory. Use the line filter to show only a couple of lines at a time when you need a gentler challenge.

٢
STEP 03

Strengthen real recall

Writing forces active memory instead of passive reading. Every correct word cements the page a little deeper — until you can write it without a single hint.

٣
